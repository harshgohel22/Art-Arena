ArtArena - Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use ArtArena.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal information such as your name and email address when you create an account.
  • Game-related data such as room codes and drawings.
  • Usage data such as your IP address and browser type for analytics purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the platform's functionality.
  • To personalize your experience and enhance gameplay.
  • To communicate with you about updates and support.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics and hosting. These services may collect and process your data in accordance with their privacy policies.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@artarena.com.
